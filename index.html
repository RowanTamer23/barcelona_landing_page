The Barcelona Vibe

Why do people fall in love with Barcelona?

It's the perfect mix: Mediterranean light, Gaudí's imagination, beaches just minutes from Gothic streets, world-class food, football passion, and a rebellious creative spirit.

When will Sagrada Família be finally finished?

Antoni Gaudí's masterpiece is very close — current estimates point to 2026–2028, more than 140 years after construction began in 1882.
